Humanity must change its relationship with nature as the planet faces three major crises: climate change, biodiversity loss and environmental pollution, according to a report on the health of the planet released by the United Nations.

根据联合国发布的一份关于地球健康状况的报告，当前地球面临着气候变化、生物多样性的丧失和环境污染三大危机，人类必须改变与自然的关系。

  • 世界面临的十大环境问题：


    气候变化：由于温室气体排放增加，导致全球气候变暖，引发极端天气事件频发、冰川融化、海平面上升等一系列连锁反应。

    Climate change: Due to the increase in greenhouse gas emissions, global warming has led to a series of chain reactions such as frequent extreme weather events, glacier melting, and rising sea levels.


    水资源短缺和污染：随着人口增长和工业活动增加，干净的饮用水日益稀缺，水体污染也日益严重。

    Water scarcity and pollution: With population growth and increased industrial activities, clean drinking water is becoming increasingly scarce, and water pollution is also becoming increasingly severe.


    生物多样性丧失：由于栖息地破坏、过度捕猎、污染等原因，许多物种面临灭绝的威胁。

    Biodiversity loss: Many species face the threat of extinction due to habitat destruction, excessive hunting, pollution, and other reasons.


    海洋污染和酸化：塑料垃圾、化学品泄漏和过度捕捞等导致海洋生态系统受到严重破坏。

    Marine pollution and acidification: Plastic waste, chemical spills, and overfishing have caused serious damage to marine ecosystems.


    城市化和土地利用变化：快速的城市扩张和不合理的土地利用导致生态系统破碎化，加剧了自然资源的消耗。

    Urbanization and land use change: Rapid urban expansion and unreasonable land use lead to ecosystem fragmentation, exacerbating the consumption of natural resources.



  • 世界面临的十大环境问题：


    空气污染：工业排放、汽车尾气、农业活动等产生的有害物质污染空气，对人类健康和生态系统造成严重影响。

    Air pollution: Harmful substances generated by industrial emissions, automobile exhaust, agricultural activities, etc. pollute the air, causing serious impacts on human health and ecosystems.


    森林砍伐：为了获得土地和木材资源，大量森林被砍伐，这不仅导致生物多样性的丧失，还加剧了气候变化。

    Deforestation: In order to obtain land and timber resources, a large amount of forests are cut down, which not only leads to the loss of biodiversity, but also exacerbates climate change.


    土壤退化和侵蚀：不合理的土地使用和农业活动导致土壤质量下降，影响粮食安全和生态平衡。

    Soil degradation and erosion: Unreasonable land use and agricultural activities lead to a decrease in soil quality, affecting food security and ecological balance.


    淡水生态系统破坏：河流、湖泊和湿地的生态系统因水利工程建设、污染和外来物种入侵等受到威胁。

    Destruction of freshwater ecosystems: The ecosystems of rivers, lakes, and wetlands are threatened by water conservancy projects, pollution, and invasive alien species.


    废弃物管理问题：随着消费水平的提高，固体废弃物和电子垃圾的产量急剧增加，处理和回收能力跟不上增长速度，导致环境污染和资源浪费。

    Waste management issues: With the improvement of consumption levels, the production of solid waste and electronic waste has sharply increased, and the processing and recycling capacity cannot keep up with the growth rate, leading to environmental pollution and resource waste.


  • 世界面临的十大环境问题之应对策略


    气候变化：减少温室气体排放：通过提高能效、发展可再生能源（如太阳能、风能）、实施碳定价和碳交易机制。

    Climate change: reducing greenhouse gas emissions: by improving energy efficiency, developing renewable energy sources (such as solar and wind), implementing carbon pricing and carbon trading mechanisms.


    水资源短缺和污染：提高水资源利用效率，推广节水技术。 加强污水处理和回收利用。 保护水源地，减少工农业污染。

    Water scarcity and pollution: improving water resource utilization efficiency and promoting water-saving technologies. Strengthen sewage treatment and recycling. Protect water sources and reduce industrial and agricultural pollution.


    生物多样性丧失：保护和恢复生态系统，建立更多的自然保护区。 实施物种保护计划，特别是对于濒危物种。

    Biodiversity loss: Protecting and restoring ecosystems, establishing more nature reserves. Implement species conservation plans, especially for endangered species.


    海洋污染和酸化：减少塑料使用和提高塑料回收率，减少塑料垃圾进入海洋。

    Marine pollution and acidification: reducing plastic usage and improving plastic recycling rates, reducing plastic waste entering the ocean.


    城市化和土地利用变化：推广绿色建筑和可持续城市规划。 保护城市周边的自然地带，限制无序扩张。

    Urbanization and land use change: promoting green buildings and sustainable urban planning. Protect the natural areas around the city and limit disorderly expansion.


  • 世界面临的十大环境问题之应对策略


    空气污染：推广使用清洁能源，减少化石燃料的使用。 加强工业排放和汽车尾气的监管。 增加绿地和城市绿化，改善城市空气质量。

    Air pollution: Promote the use of clean energy and reduce the use of fossil fuels. Strengthen the supervision of industrial emissions and automobile exhaust. Increase green space and urban greening to improve urban air quality.


    森林砍伐：实施可持续林业管理，限制非法伐木。 推广森林认证系统，鼓励消费者选择可持续来源的木材产品。 开展大规模植树造林活动。

    Deforestation: Implementing sustainable forestry management and limiting illegal logging. Promote forest certification systems and encourage consumers to choose wood products from sustainable sources. Carry out large-scale afforestation activities.


    土壤退化和侵蚀：实行可持续的农业实践，如轮作、有机耕作等。 加强土地管理，防止过度放牧和开垦。

    Soil degradation and erosion: Implementing sustainable agricultural practices such as crop rotation and organic cultivation. Strengthen land management to prevent overgrazing and cultivation.


    淡水生态系统破坏：恢复河流和湿地的自然流动和连接性。 减少农药和化肥的使用，减轻对淡水生态系统的污染。

    Destruction of freshwater ecosystems: restoring natural flow and connectivity of rivers and wetlands. Reduce the use of pesticides and fertilizers to alleviate pollution to freshwater ecosystems.


    废弃物管理问题：推广减量、再利用和回收（3R原则）。 发展循环经济，提高资源的循环利用率。

    Waste management issues: promoting reduction, reuse, and recycling (3R principle). Develop a circular economy and improve the recycling efficiency of resources.
